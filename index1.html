Product Philosophy
The most effective AI products are grounded in real user context, not generic outputs.

I believe great products are invisible — they fit so naturally into how people work and live that users stop noticing the product and start experiencing the outcome. My role is to find the right problem, design the right system, and ship the version that earns trust. Then do it again.
